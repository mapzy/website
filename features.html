Features

Mapzy focuses on doing few things really well. Our goal is that you can add a map with your stores (or any kinds of points) to your website as simply and quickly as possible. Mapzy is privacy-friendly, doesn't track your visitors or add any non-essential cookies, complying fully with GDPR.
